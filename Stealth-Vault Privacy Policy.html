At StealthVault, we take your privacy and the security of your personal data extremely seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture URL. This is used solely to create your account and manage your vault.
  • Subscription Data: We store your Premium or Pro membership status in our cloud database to ensure your features stay active across all your devices.
  • Usage Information: We collect anonymous data about how the app is used (e.g., clicks, feature usage) to improve performance and fix bugs.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as model, OS version, and unique identifiers for troubleshooting and analytics.

2. Security Features & Permissions

  • Camera: Used exclusively for Intruder Detection. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Storage: Used to encrypt and secure your personal files (photos, videos and documents) inside the vault.
  • Biometrics: Used to unlock your vault using fingerprint or Face ID.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services / Firebase
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google AdMob

These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Data Protection & Encryption

Your files are stored in a secured, encrypted environment on your device. When Cloud Sync is enabled, your subscription status is synchronized using SSL/TLS encryption with Firebase. We do not have access to your personal vault files.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the data we store about your account.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated cloud data.
  • Opt out of personalized advertisements via your device settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with an updated effective date.

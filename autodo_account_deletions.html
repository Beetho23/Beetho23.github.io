Account Deletion Request

AutoDo allows users to permanently delete their account and associated cloud data. If you wish to delete your AutoDo account, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us using the support address below.
  2. Use the subject line Delete AutoDo Account.
  3. Include the Google email address used to sign in to AutoDo.
  4. Our team will verify your request and begin the deletion process.
Request Account Deletion

Support Email

autodoandroid@gmail.com

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your Firebase Authentication account.
  • Your AutoDo cloud account information.
  • Your cloud automation profiles stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • Your synchronization history associated with your account.
  • Your profile metadata stored by AutoDo.

What Data May Be Retained?

  • Anonymous analytics collected by Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash reports that no longer identify you personally.
  • Transaction records required for legal, tax, or accounting obligations (if applicable).
  • Aggregated usage statistics that cannot identify individual users.

Deletion Time

Once your identity has been verified, your account and associated cloud data will normally be deleted within 7 business days.

Local automation profiles stored only on your device are not automatically removed from your phone. You may uninstall AutoDo or clear the application's storage if you also wish to remove local data.
